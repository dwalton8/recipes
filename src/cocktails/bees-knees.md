=== Ingredients

- ¾ ounce lemon juice
- ¾ ounce honey syrup
- 2 ounces gin

===

=== Steps

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker tin with ice.
<br>
<br>

2. Shake for 15 seconds.
<br>
<br>

3. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.
<br>
<br>

4. Garnish with lemon peal.

===
