=== Ingredients

###### Simple Syrup
- Water
- Sugar

###### Honey Syrup
- Water
- Honey

===

=== Steps

1. Add equal parts (by volume) of water and sugar (or honey) to a small pot.
<br>
<br>

2. Heat on low heat until the sugar (or honey) is fully dissolved.
<br>
<br>

3. Let cool before using.
<br>
<br>
