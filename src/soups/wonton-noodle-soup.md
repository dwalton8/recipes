=== Ingredients

- 3 cups / 750 ml chicken broth
- 200g thin wonton noodles
- 8-12 wontons depending on size
- 2 garlic cloves , smashed
- ⅓” / 1 cm piece of ginger , sliced 
- 1½ tbsp light soy sauce 
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1½ tbsp chinese cooking wine
- sesame oil to taste

===
